Motivate Me! App

This application was made in mobile platform Ionic. Ionic builds upon AngularJS and Apache Cordova to create mobile applications. You can find my source code here The goal of the application is to provide motivations to people to get tasks done that they need to get done. The goal was to start with small motivations that reward people to encourage greater accomplishments by a individual.

Motivate Me! Home

The main page of the app lists the motivations that are about to go off. So you can be reminded of them ahead of time and even complete them ahead of time or pass them off for later... It also list a shortcut to creating new motivations and tab to more places on the app.

Motivate Me! New Motivation

New Motivations page is designed to make new motivations. It requires that you put a name for the motivation, what kind of a motivation you want, when you want to be reminded, and whether or not you want to be reminded indefinitely. You can make reminders that repeat every hour, day, week, month, or every year. Providing plenty of time to be reminded.
