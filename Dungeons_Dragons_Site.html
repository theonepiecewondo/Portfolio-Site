Dungeons & Characters Website

This is a small site that was made to showcase my Dungeons and Characters app made with Flutter. This site was one of my first attempts at using bootstrap. Therefore the site is not mobile friendly, but showcases animations and parallax effect. Not a huge amount of code is in this project as it was made to showcase.

The contents of the site is about my app made in Flutter called Dungeons and Characters. The app lets you organize characters for D&D 5th edition. The max characters is unlimited. Lets you see the stats per character and more! If you want to see more about the app then you can see it here
